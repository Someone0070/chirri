The 7 Approved Pink Uses

Everything else is neutral.

1. Primary CTA Button

The single most important action on any screen. One pink button per view.

2. Landing Glassmorphic CTA

Brand hero moment. The user's first impression.

3. Floating Petals

Landing page + Garden View chirps only. Chirri's visual identity.

4. Logo / Wordmark

Brand identity. The chirri logo carries pink. Always.

5. Garden Chirp Petals (V2)

Celebratory, rare, meaningful — pink earned through event significance.

6. Empty State Accent

A whisper of pink at 15% opacity in the onboarding illustration.

7. "Most Popular" Badge

One badge on one pricing card. Small surface area, high intent.
