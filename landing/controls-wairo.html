The Color Story

How each palette interacts with the sakura pink CTA button and the overall Kyoto Studio warm background.

Cha-nezumi + Sakura
Warm earth + warm bloom. Harmonious. Like a tea house in spring.
Rikyū + Sakura
Stone + bloom. More contrast. The pink pops more against the cooler grey.
Asagi + Sakura
Sea + bloom. Complementary colors. Most visually striking but furthest from "warm."
